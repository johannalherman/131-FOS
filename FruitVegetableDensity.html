Simulation Instructions

Use this lab simulation to follow along with your Illustrated Experimental Plan for the FOS Lab Period 2 Experiment.

  1. Use the drop-down menu to select your assigned fruit or vegetable.
  2. In the top-left panel, use the piece tabs to toggle between the three pieces of your assigned fruit or vegetable. Drag the ruler within the panel to measure the three dimensions of each piece individually. Each piece will be displayed in three ways, so that all three dimensions of the object can be measured.
  3. In the top-right panel, use the buttons along the bottom to control the items on the balance.
  4. In the bottom-left panel, use the ADD buttons below the beaker sitting on a balance to add one of your assigned fruit or vegetable pieces, solid sodium chloride, and water into the beaker.
  5. In the bottom-left panel, use the add/remove buttons below the graduated cylinder sitting on a balance to transfer portions of your prepared solution into the cylinder.

You can restart the simulation at any time by clicking on the Reset All Controls button.
